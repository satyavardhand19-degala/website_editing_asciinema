📖 How to Use

📂 1. Upload Select your .cast file. The video player will load automatically.
✂️ 2. Select & Keep Drag the blue handles to select a part you want to KEEP. Click ✨CUT to save that selection.
🎨 3. Customize Adjust the Width/Height or change the Speed of the video using the dropdown.
💾 4. Export Click Preview to check your edits. If satisfied, click Export .cast.
